Why Do You Need to Add Date on Photo?

According to the official notifications of the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) and Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), the photograph uploaded by the candidate must:

  • Not be more than 3 months old.
  • Clearly show the Date on which the photograph has been taken.
  • Be in JPEG/JPG format.
  • Have a file size between 20 KB to 50 KB.
  • Have standard dimensions of 3.5 cm (width) x 4.5 cm (height).
Warning: Applications without the date printed on the photo are often rejected during the scrutiny process. Don't take a risk with your career. Use our tool to fix your photo in 5 seconds.
